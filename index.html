8 QR Types for every need

Whatever your use case — restaurant menus, business cards, WiFi sharing, or event check-ins — Trisend has the right QR format.

🔗
URL / Website
Link to any website, product page, or landing page. Most popular QR type.
📶
WiFi Access
Share WiFi credentials instantly. No more typing passwords for customers.
